Interests

There are only three things I have strong feelings about in this world and they are: peanut butter, anime, and dogs. I love all three in equal measure.

Peanut Butter

My favorite food is peanut butter. Peanut butter and jelly sandwiches, peanut butter cookies, peanut butter fudge—I love them all. I like it because of its sweet taste and versatility. In my opinion, smooth peanut butter is the best…even if I don’t always have the best luck opening the jar…

Anime

Anime refers to Japanese animated productions that are often characterized by colorful graphics, vibrant characters, and intricate storylines. Anime covers a wide range of genres, including action, romance, comedy, and drama, and is enjoyed by people of all ages around the world. Many anime series have gained a devoted following, and the medium has become an important part of popular culture in many countries.

I grew up watching Inu Yasha, Dragon Ball, and Naruto, but these days I tend to like (unfortunately) shorter anime like Chainsaw Man and One Punch Man. However, one of my favorite characters of all time is from Naruto. His name is Uchiha Madara.

Dogs

Needless to say, I’m a dog person. I have only ever had one dog in my life, but she was my best friend while I had her and my memories of her remain precious to this day. You can learn more about my friend, Tiffany, in Family.

In general, I like dogs because of their loyalty, affection, and playfulness. I prefer big dogs, because they don’t generally bark as much as small dogs do, and they’re usually more calm. That said, the one downside is that I can’t easily pick up big dogs and I like that aspect in small dogs.
