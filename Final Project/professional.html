Professional

I have not had many jobs, but one of the more rewarding jobs I have had was as an English teacher in a small Japanese village called Ouchi.

Here are some of the goodbye cards my students wrote for me:

Dear Kayla,

Thank you for everything up until now. I disliked English class, but ever since you arrived, I’ve come to like it. Thank you. I was happy because I said “Hello” to you and you said “Hello” back. Your clothes were always so cute. From now on, especially as a 5th grader, I’ll give English my all.

From,

Inuyama Airi
